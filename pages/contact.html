Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Booking & Reservations

How far in advance can I book?

You can book up to 30 days in advance for all room types.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are free up to 2 hours before your booking time.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 5 or more people.

Facilities & Services

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we provide high-speed Wi-Fi free of charge to all visitors.

Do you have parking?

Yes, we have dedicated parking spaces for our customers.

Are food and drinks allowed?

Yes, but only in designated areas to maintain cleanliness.

Membership & Payment

How does the credit system work?

You can add credit to your account and use it for all services.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, we offer 15% discount for students with valid ID.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments.
