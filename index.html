About Us

Sigma Study House is a youth-led project dedicated to providing the ideal study environment for students by offering quiet, fully equipped private study rooms and meeting spaces.

We believe that the right space makes a real difference in the quality of learning, so we've created areas that combine comfort, calm, and the modern tools every student or study group needs.

Located in Amman, Jordan, our facility offers a variety of study environments tailored to different needs and preferences, from silent individual rooms to collaborative group spaces.
